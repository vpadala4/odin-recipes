Ingredients

  • -50g Cornflakes
  • Secret Herbs & Spices: Black Pepper, White Pepper, Salt, Thyme, Basil, Oregano, Celery Salt, Paprika, Mustard Powder, Garlic Powder, Ginger Powder
  • 475g Raw Chicken Breast
  • 2 Beaten/Whisked Eggs
  • KFC Pepper Mayo: 50g Light or Fat Free Mayo, Squeeze Of Lemon Juice & Ground Black Pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Mini Flatbreads: (or you can use store bought flatbreads / mini tortillas)
  • 120g All Purpose Flour (Brand: Vetta Smart Plain Protein Flour)
  • 1.5 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 100g Low Fat Greek Yoghurt
  • Salt

Steps To Prepare

  1. Crush cornflakes into small pieces
  2. Add a dash of what you have available from Secret Herbs and Spices
  3. Cut chicken breast into 5 pieces - 95g each
  4. Dip chicken in Whisked Egg and coat in cornflakes
  5. Spray light cooking oil spray
  6. Air Fry at 190 C for 16-18 min OR Bake at 200 C for 20- 25 min
  7. For Mayo,mix together light mayo,lemon juice and pepper
  8. For Flatbreads
    • Mix flour,baking powder,yogurt and salt together
    • Knead it to form a dough and roll it out on flat surface
    • Cut out five mini flatbreads
    • Cook each side on medium heat for 2-3 min
  9. Assemble each slider and enjoy
